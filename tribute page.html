William Shakespeare

The Bard of Avon (1564 - 1616)

Biography

William Shakespeare,the legendary playwright and poet,was born in 1564 in Startford-Upon-Avon.He wrote 39 plays and 154 sonnets that continue to captivate the audiences worldwide.

Famous Quotes

" Some are born great, some achieve greatness, and some have greatness thrust upon them. "
" To be, or not to be: that is the question. "

Notable Works

Hamlet (1603)-" To be, or not to be. "

Macbeth (1606)-" Fair is foul and foul is fair. "

Romeo & Juliet (1597) -" Parting is such a sweet sorrow."

Shakespeare's Legacy

His words shaped mordern storytelling,inspiring countless adaptation,movies and book.His influence is seen in literature,theater and film even today.
